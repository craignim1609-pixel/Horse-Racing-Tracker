Quick Start

ACCA

  • Add a selection to the Acca: Use New Pick to add legs to the accumulator.
  • Update the Acca: As results come in, use the buttons on the Accumulator page to mark each leg.
  • Complete the Acca: When all legs are settled, press Complete Acca to save it to history.

Race Day

  • Add your daily selections: Use New Pick to enter as many picks as you like.
  • Track the day: Update each pick’s result on the Race Day page as races finish.
  • Complete the day: When all races are done, press Complete Day to lock in the results.

Tip: Export your PDF or Excel reports at the end of each day to keep a clean record of your performance.
