Quick Start

Get up and running in a few simple steps:

  1. Add Picks: Go to New Pick and enter today’s selections.
  2. Track the Day: Use the Race Day page to update results as races finish.
  3. Build Accas: Add legs on the Accumulator page and save completed accas.
  4. Complete & Export: Complete the race day, then export PDF or Excel if needed.
  5. Review Performance: Open the Stats page to see long‑term results and ROI.

Tip: Use the PDF and Excel exports after each day to keep a clean record of your betting history.
